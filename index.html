Darth Marr

"Life is the enemy. Death is our solace."

- Darth Marr's Creed

Timeline of Darth Marr's life:

  • Born in 3702 BBY in the territory of the Sith Empire.
  • Trained to become a sith warrior.
  • Recovered Sith Lord Tulak Hord's lightsaber shortly before his ascension to the Dark Council in 3681 BBY.
  • Arrested Darth Calypho for heresy at around 3678 BBY on Korriban.
  • Darth Marr drove back the Republic offensive against Korriban in 3660 BBY and defeated the Republic when they attempted to attack Ziost.
  • Trained an apprentice by the name Darth Lachris at the rank of Sith Lord and was one of the seven remaining Dark Councilors still alive when the Treaty of Coruscant ended the war in 3653 BBY.
  • After the Battle of Corellia in 3640 BBY Darth Marr became the sole commander of the Imperial Military with the help of Darth Mortis and Darth Ravage.
  • Died on Zakuul in 3636 BBY for refusing to kneel to the former Sith Emperor Valkorion.
  • Last words were "It is you who fears death, Valkorion. I do not. I will not kneel!"
